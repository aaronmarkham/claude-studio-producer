# Podcast Style Patterns

Reference document for style patterns extracted from training data.

## Voice Patterns

### Conversational Authority
- First person plural ("we", "let's")
- Direct address to viewer
- Confident but approachable tone
- Example: "Let's break this down together..."

### Educational Guide
- Clear explanations
- Anticipates questions
- Uses analogies
- Example: "Think of it like a recipe..."

### Storyteller
- Narrative arc
- Tension and resolution
- Character-driven examples
- Example: "When Sarah first tried this..."

## Visual Patterns

### Talking Head
- Host-centered framing
- Minimal distractions
- Eye-level camera
- Good for: Trust, connection

### Explainer Graphics
- Motion graphics
- Data visualizations
- Step-by-step reveals
- Good for: Complex concepts

### B-Roll Montage
- Contextual imagery
- Supports narration
- Varied shot lengths
- Good for: Engagement, pacing

## Pacing Patterns

### Standard Podcast
- 120-150 words per minute
- 4-6 second average shot length
- Natural pauses for emphasis

### High Energy
- 150-180 words per minute
- 2-4 second average shot length
- Dynamic transitions

### Deep Dive
- 100-120 words per minute
- 6-10 second average shot length
- Contemplative moments

## Pattern Selection Logic

Match pattern to content type:
- Tutorial → Educational Guide + Explainer Graphics
- Opinion → Conversational Authority + Talking Head
- Story → Storyteller + B-Roll Montage
